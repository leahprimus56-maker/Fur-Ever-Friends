Max

Five days ago, a man came to our shelter looking for a small dog. While waiting to meet one, a volunteer brought out a 60-pound, shy dog found in the woods. This dog, Max, walked straight up to the man, put his front legs on his lap, and looked at him. "So much for a small dog," the man thought. Max had been at the shelter for three months, but he instantly found his forever home that day.
