Volunteering/Donating

"Running a sanctuary like Fur-Ever Friends is a community effort fueled by love. Every donation—no matter the size—directly funds life-saving medical care, high-quality nutrition, and the cozy bedding that makes our shelter feel like a home. However, our residents need more than just supplies; they need the warmth of a human touch. By volunteering your time, you provide the socialization and play that helps our pets regain their confidence, making them much more likely to find their forever families. Whether you give through your wallet or your time, you are the reason we can keep our doors open and our tails wagging."

Frequently Asked Questions

How old do I need to be to volunteer?

For safety reasons, volunteers must be at least 16 years old. Volunteers under 18 will need a signed parental consent form before their first shift.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes! Fur-Ever Friends is a registered non-profit organization. You will receive a digital receipt via email immediately after donating for your records.

Can I donate physical items instead of money?

Absolutely! We are always in need of kitten food, gently used blankets, and cleaning supplies. Please refer to our Wish List above for current priorities.

Where does my donation go?

All funds go to providing our furry friends with nutritious meals, essential medical care such as vaccinations and surgery and to also give our new adopters a "Gotcha Day" care package.
